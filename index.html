Read Introduction

WXDU is the student-run radio station of Duke University. It was first broadcasted in 1950 on the AM frequency 650. Today, it is kept running by small and ever-changing group of DJs. DJ's are encouraged to play from the station's physical library, which grows with new albums added each week.

'Hidden Track' is a series of recollections from various DJs at the station, some old and some new. I've attempted to capture the memory of WXDU through both its 'sound' and the personal experiences of the DJs that have shaped that sound, so their recorded recollections are interspersed with songs they have once played on the air.

To listen to the playlist without the recorded recollections, simply toggle the 'dialogue' button on the bottom left of the playlist.

To see the full playlist, click to 'arrow' button on the top left-hand corner.

Disclaimer: I do not own the songs in the playlist. They belong to their respective owners.
